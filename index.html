RESEARCH OVERVIEW

We are an interdisciplinary research group that is broadly interested in understanding how the physical and chemical nature of macromolecules and supramolecular polymers can shape the structure of aqueous solutions, construct spatial-temporally controlled functions in the structured aqueous solution and be applied to build ordered and dynamic biomedical or optoelectronic materials. The main research directions are as following.

MACROMOLECULES AND SUPRAMOLECULAR POLYMERS

HIERARCHICAL AQUEOUS STRUCTURES

IMAGING DROPLETS IN MICROFLUIDIC DEVICES WITH CONFOCAL LASER SCANNING MICROSCOPE
